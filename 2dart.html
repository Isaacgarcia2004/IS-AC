S M I L E (2025)

This piece was created during my time at community college, Cabrillo College in Aptos specifically. For this project, we were told to go out into campus and take note of anything interesting we found. Walking around the ceramics department, I found this, half of a smiley face, so I drew it into my sketchbook. When I got home, I put my sketch into Procreate and finished the drawing digitally.
